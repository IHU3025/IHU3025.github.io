Selfie: The Wrong Way vs. The Right Way

The prespective distortion is due to the exaggerated perspective effect. When the object is closer to the phone, the phone's camera uses a short focal length (wide-angle lens) with a large Field of View. Since the object distance is small, the relative distance from lens to the closes feature vs. lens to furthest feature is large. Hence this large relative distance result the closer features to be rendered disproportionately larger than the further one, resulting in perspective distortion. On the other hand, when the picture zoomed in, phone's camera adjust to a telephoto or portrait lens setting, you compensate for stepping back and keep the face the same size in the frame. And since the relative difference is samll, facial featuers does not have tis exaggerated effect.
